Service Overview

What Is Temp-to-Perm Hiring?

Temp-to-perm — also called contract-to-hire — is an arrangement where a worker begins in a temporary role with the explicit understanding that strong performance leads to a permanent offer. It bridges the gap between short-term staffing and long-term commitment.

Talent Pull Inc. handles the entire temp period as employer of record: payroll, WSIB, and compliance. Once you decide to convert, we manage the transition smoothly so the candidate becomes your direct employee with no disruption.

Why Choose Temp-to-Perm

The Advantages for Your Business

Real Performance Data

You see how a candidate actually works — attendance, attitude, productivity — not just how they interview. Make hire/no-hire decisions based on facts.

Reduced Turnover

Permanent hires made after a temp period are significantly more likely to stay long-term — both employer and employee have validated the fit.

Zero Compliance Risk

During the temp period, Talent Pull is the employer of record. WSIB, payroll, and employment standards obligations are all ours, not yours.

Lower Cost-Per-Hire

Avoiding a bad permanent hire — which typically costs 1–3× annual salary in retraining and rehiring — makes temp-to-perm one of the most cost-effective approaches.

Team Culture Fit

Observe how candidates integrate with your existing team, take direction, and adapt to your workplace culture before making a long-term offer.

Fast Onboarding

The candidate is already trained, oriented, and productive before conversion — meaning zero ramp-up delay when they transition to your payroll.
