Service Overview

What Is Temporary Staffing?

Temporary staffing provides businesses with pre-screened workers on a short-term or fixed-duration basis. The staffing agency, Talent Pull Inc., remains the employer of record, handling payroll, WSIB, and compliance so you can focus entirely on operations.

Whether you need to cover seasonal demand, replace absent staff, or staff up a new production line, our team deploys job-ready candidates across Ontario within 24 to 72 hours of a confirmed request.

Use Cases

When Do Businesses Use Temporary Staffing?

Seasonal Demand Spikes

Peak production seasons, holiday rushes, or large contract wins that require rapid headcount growth without long-term commitments.

Employee Absences & Leaves

Covering medical leave, vacations, or unexpected absences; ensuring production lines and operations never slow down.

Short-Term Projects

Specific project-based work with a defined end date; warehouse reorganization, production runs, or temporary line expansion.

New Facility Ramp-Up

Opening a new plant, warehouse, or department and need a trained workforce on site quickly before finalizing permanent hires.

Evaluate Before You Commit

Use the temporary engagement to assess a candidate's fit before converting to a permanent hire; reducing long-term turnover risk.

Reduce Payroll Overhead

Shift workforce costs from fixed to variable. Pay only for hours worked, with no benefits, vacation, or termination liability.
