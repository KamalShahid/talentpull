Service Overview

What Is Permanent Recruitment?

Permanent recruitment — also called direct-hire or full-time placement — is the process of sourcing, evaluating, and placing a candidate into a long-term role within your organization. Unlike temporary staffing, the hired individual becomes your employee from day one.

At Talent Pull Inc., our permanent recruitment service covers the entire hiring cycle: job profiling, active sourcing, multi-stage screening, reference checks, and onboarding support — so you can focus on running your business while we find the right fit.

Our Process

How Our Permanent Recruitment Works

A transparent, structured process designed to reduce time-to-hire and improve long-term retention.

1

Discovery & Role Profiling

We meet with your team to understand the role, required skills, team culture, and growth expectations. A detailed candidate profile ensures every submission is on-target.

2

Active Sourcing & Outreach

We tap our existing talent network and use multi-channel sourcing — job boards, LinkedIn, referrals, and AI-assisted search — to identify both active and passive candidates.

3

Screening, Interviews & Verification

Shortlisted candidates complete structured interviews, skills assessments, and reference checks. We verify credentials, employment history, and background where required.

4

Presentation & Client Interviews

We present a shortlist of 2–4 qualified candidates with detailed profiles. We coordinate interview scheduling, gather feedback, and support your evaluation process.

5

Offer, Placement & Onboarding Support

We assist with offer negotiation, acceptance, and notice period coordination. Post-placement follow-up ensures a smooth start for both the candidate and your team.
