About Talent Pull Inc.

Get to Work with the Leading Recruitment Agency in Canada

Based in Burlington, Ontario, Talent Pull Inc. was built on a simple belief: great businesses deserve great people, and great people deserve great opportunities. We combine deep industry knowledge with AI-powered sourcing to place the right candidates — faster and more reliably than traditional staffing.

  • End-to-end recruitment support: from job postings and screening to onboarding

  • Full WSIB coverage and Ontario employment standards compliance on every placement

  • Access to a wide pool of pre-screened, job-ready candidates available immediately
