Ontario OHSA Compliance

Why Safety Training Is Legally Required in Ontario

Under the Ontario Occupational Health and Safety Act (OHSA), employers have a legal obligation to provide workers with the information, instruction, and supervision necessary to protect their health and safety. Failure to comply can result in OHSA orders, fines, and prosecution.

Talent Pull ensures every temporary and permanent worker is trained before their first shift, protecting your business from liability and your workers from preventable injury.
